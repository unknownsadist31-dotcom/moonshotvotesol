Rewards Moonshot XP

  • Moonshot XP is your on-platform reputation and contribution score. You earn XP by participating in the Moonshot ecosystem — discovering new assets, voting, engaging with launches, and completing in-app quests.
  • We plan to convert Moonshot XP into the MOON token via a future reward event. Exact mechanics (conversion rate, eligibility windows, snapshots, and distribution) will be announced publicly ahead of time.
  • The intent is simple: early community members should own a piece of the network they help create. XP tracks meaningful participation without requiring complex crypto flows up front — and later maps that effort to MOON in a transparent way.

What to expect (high level):

  • Announcement: we'll publish a formal MOON rewards post with timelines and criteria.
  • Eligibility: XP earned through verified, non-abusive actions will count toward conversion.
  • Fairness: anti-spam and anti-Sybil checks apply; suspicious XP may be excluded.
  • Transparency: methodology and snapshots will be documented before distribution.

* This page is informational only and does not constitute an offer or solicitation. The XP→MOON plan may evolve and is subject to compliance review and market conditions. Participation is not financial advice; do your own research.
